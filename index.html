Udaipur


I recently had a wonderful two-day trip to Udaipur. The city was absolutely beautiful and filled with unforgettable experiences. I started by renting a bike, which made exploring the city so much fun. My first stop was the magnificent City Palace, followed by a visit to the serene Lake Pichola. Both places were breathtaking and truly showcased Udaipur's charm.

Next, I embarked on a journey to **Bahubali Hill**, which involved a short trek of about 2-3 kilometers. The trek was refreshing, and the view from the top was stunning. To add to the experience, I enjoyed a hot bowl of Maggi at the summit, which felt perfect in that setting.

After that, I headed to the **ropeway station** to try the cable car ride. It was an amazing experience, offering panoramic views of the city. Finally, I spent some time in the **local market**, exploring shops filled with beautiful handicrafts, traditional dresses, and other unique items.

Every moment of the trip was delightful, and Udaipur's vibrant culture and scenic beauty made it truly memorable.
